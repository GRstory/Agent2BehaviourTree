The overall strategy was reactive and lacked adaptability.

## Improvement Suggestions
1.  **Prioritize Ice Spell on Scan:** Modify the IceSpell sequence to execute *immediately* after a successful scan, regardless of the enemy's weakness.  Remove the condition: `IsTurnEarly(2)` as this is irrelevant.
2.  **Dynamic MP Management:** Introduce a secondary condition to the IceSpell sequence: `HasMP(15)`. This will prevent the BT from repeatedly scanning when MP is low, and instead prioritize Ice spells when MP is available.
3.  **Heal Trigger Adjustment:** Change the heal trigger to `IsPlayerHPLow(60)` instead of `IsPlayerHPLow(40)`. This will trigger the heal action earlier, potentially mitigating damage before it becomes critical.
4. **Defend Trigger Adjustment:** Change the Defend trigger to `EnemyIsTelegraphing(HeavySlam)` and add a delay of 2 turns after the telegraph. This allows the player to react to the telegraph and reduces the chance of being caught off guard.
