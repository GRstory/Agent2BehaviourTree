## Analysis
The combat consistently resulted in a drawn-out, unwinnable scenario. The primary issue was a reliance on scanning and then attempting to exploit a weakness (Fire) with Fire spells, while the FireGolem’s high HP and resistance to Fire meant the player was constantly reacting defensively. The BT lacked a proactive strategy to quickly reduce the enemy’s health, and the frequent defensive actions (Defend, Heal) drained resources without significantly impacting the fight. The delayed use of Ice, even when the enemy was scanned, was a critical missed opportunity. The BT prioritized scanning over immediate damage output.

## Improvement Suggestions
1. **Prioritize Ice Spell on Scan:** Modify the `IceSpell()` task to be triggered immediately upon scanning the enemy and confirming the Ice weakness, regardless of HP thresholds. This should override other defensive tasks during the scan phase.
2. **Aggressive Early Response:** Introduce a new condition: `IsEnemyHPHigh(80)`. If this condition is true, immediately trigger the `Attack()` task, bypassing the defensive sequences. This will help to break the enemy’s momentum.
3. **Adjust Heal Threshold:** Increase the HP threshold for the `Heal()` task from 40% to 60%. This allows for more proactive healing when the player is closer to critical health, rather than waiting until they are almost dead.
4. **Reduce Scan Frequency:** Reduce the scan frequency. Scanning every turn is too slow.  Implement a scan on turn 1, then every 3 turns. This conserves MP and allows for more immediate action.
5. **Introduce a "Counter-Attack" Task:** Add a simple attack task triggered when the enemy is telegraphing a heavy attack, but *before* the Defend task. This provides a quick, reactive damage option when the enemy is preparing a strong attack, rather than solely relying on a defensive posture.
