The attack every 3 turns was ineffective given the enemy’s aggressive nature and the lack of proactive scanning. The BT’s reliance on scanning before spellcasting was also a weakness, as the FireGolem didn’t telegraph its attacks reliably enough for this to be a reliable strategy.

## Improvement Suggestions
1.  **Prioritize Scanning:** Implement scanning on every turn (turn 1). This allows for continuous enemy assessment and immediate exploitation of weaknesses.
2.  **Conditional Spellcasting Based on Scan Results:** Modify the 'IceSpell' and 'FireSpell' tasks. Similarly, the FireSpell should only trigger after a scan and confirmation of the FireGolem’s Fire weakness.
3.  **Dynamic Defend Trigger:** Adjust the 'Defend' task's trigger condition. Instead of solely relying on the enemy telegraphing a HeavySlam, incorporate a broader trigger: "EnemyHPBelow(60)" – this would allow for defensive action regardless of telegraphing, providing a more immediate response to damage.
4. **Resource Prioritization:**  Introduce a resource management layer.  If the scan reveals a weakness, prioritize MP usage for the corresponding spell.  Implement a mechanism to automatically reduce TP usage if a spell isn’t used due to MP depletion.
5. **Reduce Attack Frequency:**  Remove the "Attack every 3 turns" task. The BT should focus on reacting to the enemy’s actions rather than following a rigid timing schedule.
