4. **Heal Threshold Adjustment:** Increase the heal threshold from 70% to 60%. This allows the player to heal earlier, potentially mitigating damage and allowing for more strategic spellcasting.
5. **Introduce a "Delay" Task:** Add a "Delay" task after the scan. This gives the BT a brief pause (1-2 turns) to fully process the scan results before executing any other action, allowing for more informed decision-making.
